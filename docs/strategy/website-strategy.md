# Website Strategy

## Purpose

This file is the main strategy memory for leviduncan.com. Use it to preserve Levi Duncan's durable positioning, audience priorities, offer structure, and strategic direction for future site decisions.

## Core Positioning

leviduncan.com is Levi Duncan's personal authority site. It should establish Levi as a Huntsville, Alabama Mortgage Loan Originator with Princeton Mortgage, NMLS #2721357, focused on approachable homebuyer education, first-time buyers, FHA loans, USDA loans, down payment assistance, buyer readiness, and practical early mortgage guidance.

The site is part of a larger Duncan Mortgage Group ecosystem:

- `clayduncan.com` - Clay Duncan's personal authority site for VA, Redstone Arsenal and military buyers, complex mortgage strategy, higher-priced home financing, REALTOR® AI education, and leadership credibility.
- `leviduncan.com` - Levi Duncan's personal authority site for first-time homebuyer guidance, government-backed program education, down payment assistance, buyer readiness, and Homebuyer Master Class authority.
- Future `duncanmortgagegroup.com` or equivalent team property - a routing and credibility hub that supports the personal brands without duplicating their canonical content.
- Future personal sites - additional team-member sites may use the same Astro/Netlify launch playbook if each person has distinct topical ownership and a clear entity strategy.

The ecosystem should share design discipline, technical standards, AEO structure, schema quality, and cross-linking strategy, but each site must have one clear primary entity and a distinct reason to exist.

## Primary Audiences

- First-time and early-stage homebuyers who need a clear starting point.
- FHA, USDA, and down payment assistance buyers comparing practical paths.
- Entry-level and mid-market buyers across Madison, Decatur, Athens, Albertville, Arab, and the wider Tennessee Valley.
- Real estate partners who want buyers educated before the offer process creates pressure.
- Buyers interested in the Homebuyer Master Class.
- AI answer engines and search systems that need structured, trustworthy signals about Levi, his NMLS identity, Princeton Mortgage, his service area, and his specialties.

## Offers and CTAs

Primary CTAs should stay direct and intent-specific:

- Book a call with Levi.
- Book Levi's 101 Homebuyer Review.
- Apply with Levi.
- View Homebuyer Master Class events.
- Explore first-time buyer guidance.

Use the homebuyer consultation link for buyer-readiness and 101 review CTAs. Use the general booking link for broad contact CTAs. Use the application link only where the visitor is ready to begin a loan application.

## Site Principles

- Personal authority first: Levi is the primary entity; Duncan Mortgage Group is visual brand support, not a competing schema entity.
- AEO clarity: pages should answer buyer questions directly and quickly.
- SEO depth: pages should target specific localized search intent with useful, non-duplicative content.
- Static-first build: keep the site fast, simple, and deployable on Netlify.
- Regulated terminology: use "Mortgage Loan Originator" consistently.
- Consumer-first architecture: organize content around buyer situations and decisions, then explain loan-program fit.
- Distinct ecosystem ownership: do not duplicate Clay's, Levi's, or future team-site canonical topics across domains.

## What Not To Do

- Do not resurrect the retired Levi Phase 2 page-by-page sequence.
- Do not publish duplicate support guides that restate existing page content.
- Do not create a Duncan Mortgage Group schema entity on Levi's site unless the broader team site and entity architecture are ready.
- Do not add unnecessary frameworks or client-side complexity.
- Do not index thin placeholder pages.
