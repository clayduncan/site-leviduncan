## Content Rules

- Do not index placeholder pages.
- Use official sources for regulated or program-specific claims.
- Keep content useful to humans first, while structured for answer engines.
- Do not duplicate Clay/Levi/team pages across domains.
- Write around consumer situations first, then explain loan programs.
- Preserve AEO structure on every important page: direct answer, audience, offer, proof, service area, next step, FAQs, sources, and schema-ready layout.
